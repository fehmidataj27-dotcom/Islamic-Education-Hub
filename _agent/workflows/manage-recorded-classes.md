# Recorded Classes Management Workflow

Follow these steps to manage educational video content in the Islamic Education Hub.

## 1. Adding a New Recorded Lecture
-   Navigate to the **Recorded Classes** page via the sidebar.
-   As an Admin/Teacher, click the **Add Lecture** button (+ icon) at the top right.
-   Fill in the required details:
    -   **Title (English & Urdu)**: Provide clear titles for both languages.
    -   **Subject**: Select the category (Tajweed, Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, etc.). The card will automatically use a premium subject-specific graphic.
    -   **Duration**: Enter the video length (e.g., "45 min").
    -   **Media File**: Upload the video or audio file.
-   Click **Save Lecture**. The system will upload the file and create the record.

## 2. Filtering and Viewing Lectures
-   Use the **Subject Filter** dropdown at the top to sort videos by category.
-   Click **Watch Now** on any card to open the integrated Media Player.
-   Progress bars on each card indicate how much of the lecture has been completed.

## 3. Deleting a Lecture
-   Hover over a video card to reveal the **Delete** (Trash) icon (Admin only).
-   Click the icon and confirm the action in the pop-up dialog.
-   The lecture and its associated file will be removed from the system.

## 4. Visual Themes
Each course category has a unique premium theme:
-   **Tajweed**: Emerald Green with Mosque icon.
-   **Tafseer**: Rose Red with Holy Book icon.
-   **Hadith**: Violet/Purple with Antique Jar icon.
-   **Fiqh**: Indigo Blue with Scales of Justice icon.
-   **Namaz**: Slate/Dark with Praying Hands icon.
-   **Arabic**: Teal with Pen icon.
-   **History**: Amber/Orange with Scroll icon.
